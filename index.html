Psyche Asteroid

Exploring a metal-rich world in space

What is the Psyche Asteroid?

Psyche (officially called 16 Psyche) is a large asteroid located in the main asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. Unlike most asteroids, Psyche is believed to be made largely of metal, mainly iron and nickel. Scientists think it could be the exposed core of an early planet.

Why is Psyche Important?

Psyche is unique because studying it may help scientists understand how planetary cores form. If Psyche really is a planetary core, it gives us a rare opportunity to study the inside of a planet without drilling.

NASA's Psyche Mission

NASA launched the Psyche Mission to study this asteroid up close. The spacecraft will orbit Psyche and analyze its composition, magnetic field, and surface features to learn more about early solar system history.
